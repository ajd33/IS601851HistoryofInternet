April 14th, 2000

This is known as Black Friday, the day the dot com bubble popped and the NASDAQ fell 350 points. By 9/11, the NASDAQ lost 2/3 of it's value.

May 1997

By now, Netscape, Yahoo, Excite, and finally Amazon are all publicly traded. This is the height of the dot com bubble.

1994

Netscape launches in Silicon Valley and Navigator hits the streets and is a major success. This is the beginning of the browser war.

1989

While working at CERN, the World Wide Web is invented by Tim Berners-Lee.
